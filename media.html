Biodiversity “the biological diversity of all life on Earth”... The purpose of this website is to raise awareness of what biodiversity is and the current threats in Aotearoa using bold photographs and verified data. Human activity has pushed (and is still pushing) many species to the brink of extinction, this website will endeavour to keep up to date information (and or links to other sources i.e. IUCN*) detailing current threats and statuses of native species, ecosystems and habitats. (*The IUCN red list rates all the threatened species on the planet).
